  • Desktop release with auto-update, sandbox conlang mode, and a public download site

Bingery

AI-powered anime discovery and tracking platform — a conversational guide that understands taste ("recommend me something like Re:Zero"), backed by a custom similarity engine and a studio-grade design system. Live as an installable PWA.
